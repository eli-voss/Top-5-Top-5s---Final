Movie Series

Spider-Man
 Favorite superhero brought into great cinema. No bad version of spider man. Favorite is a tough choice between Tobey Maguire and Miles Morales.
Harry Potter
 Great fantasy series with great characters and immersivity. Fun to watch and has some amazing moments.
Star Wars
 Great sci-fi series with iconic moments and characters. Fun to theorize about and explore the complexity of its universe.
Lord of the Rings
 Amazing fantasy story with rich lore and unforgettable characters. A masterpiece of storytelling and cinematic achievement.
Avengers
 Great modern superhero collaboration with a great cast and an impressive budget.

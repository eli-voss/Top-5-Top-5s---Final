Video Games

A list comprised of my top 5 favorite played video games across various platforms and genres based on personal preference and impact.

Competitive Multiplayer

Rocket League
 Easily one of the best competitive multiplayer games, combining an incredible esports atmosphere with fast-paced gameplay with an extremely high skill ceiling.
Rainbow Six Siege
 A tactical team-based bomb defusal shooter with unique operators and realistic abilities and map destructibility, paired with a very competitive esports scene grants it the number two spot on my list.
Valorant
 A tactical team-based bomb defusal shooter with unique agents and insane abilities, blending precise gunplay with an insane esports scene and an amazing anti-cheat system gives Valorant the #3 sport on my list.
Counter-Strike 2
 The most basic, down to earth competitive multiplayer shooter, Counter-Strike 2 focuses on pure gunplay and map control, making it a timeless classic in the esports world that will not be replaced.
Super Smash Bros. Ultimate
 Generational platform fighting game that features all of Nintendo's most iconic characters, perfect for parties and tournaments. First of its kind and cannot be replaced.

Casual

Minecraft
 The ultimate sandbox; endlessly creative and accessible, play solo or with friends to build, survive, and explore any world you imagine, or play games on a world someone has created. Highest setlling video game of all time for a reason.
Terraria
 2D Minecraft meets Metroidvania; endless exploration, crafting, and combat in a procedurally generated world full of secrets and surprises. Much better progression than Minecraft, but due to preference for 3D games it takes the #2 spot on my list.
Skate
 Great game to kick back and relax with, the open world and fluid skateboarding mechanics make for a very enjoyable experience. Generational skateboarding game that is hard to put down.
Battlefield 6
 Tanks, planes, and large scale battles make for a very fun casual multiplayer experience with friends. The large maps and vehicle combat set it apart from other shooters.
Peak
 Its peak.

Story

Elden Ring
 First souls-like I've ever played, its story's complexity is hard to beat on top of impeccable gameplay and bossfights.
Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice
 Great story with even better gameplay, Sekiro's unique combat system and challenging bosses make for an unforgettable experience.
The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time
 I would've put all of The Legend of Zelda on this list, but thought to narrow it down to my favorite. As one of the most influential video games ever, Ocarina of Time's timeless adventure, memorable characters, and rich world defined not only an era, but an entire series.
The Last of Us
 A gripping post-apocalyptic tale of survival and humanity, The Last of Us combines emotional storytelling with intense gameplay, creating an unforgettable experience.
Undertale
 Amazing story, but even better boss fights and music that is impossible to forget. Multiple endings based on player choices make for a unique experience each time.
